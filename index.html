Welcome to Nabastic

Our Story

Born in the heart of Kolkata, we're a streetwear brand ignited by a passion for self-expression. Inspired by the city's vibrant energy and diverse culture, we create bold designs that empower individuals to be their authentic selves.

Our Mission

To inspire and equip young people in India with the confidence to express themselves through bold, culturally relevant streetwear.

Our Vision

To be the leading global streetwear brand that redefines self-expression, empowering individuals to confidently embrace their individuality.
